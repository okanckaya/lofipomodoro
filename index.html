Privacy Policy – Lofi Pomodoro

This Privacy Policy explains how the Lofi Pomodoro app handles your data. Short version: Lofi Pomodoro does not collect, store or share any personal data from your device. Timer preferences and settings stay on your device and are never sent to any external server by the app.

Data we do not collect

Lofi Pomodoro does not:

  • Collect your name, email address or contact details inside the app
  • Collect your focus sessions, tasks, notes or any other in-app content
  • Collect location data
  • Collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Track your behavior across other apps or websites
  • Use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs or tracking tools

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Data stored on your device

Any settings you adjust inside Lofi Pomodoro — such as timer lengths, sound options or ambience choices — are stored locally on your device.

This data is under your control:

  • You can reset or change settings from inside the app where options exist
  • You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app

Lofi Pomodoro does not transmit this local data to any server operated by the developer.

Purchases

Lofi Pomodoro may offer an optional in-app purchase such as a “Buy me a coffee” tip. These transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. The app itself does not receive your full payment details. Apple may process basic information related to your purchase in accordance with Apple’s own privacy policy and App Store terms.

Support contact

If you contact me by email at okanckaya@gmail.com, I will receive the information you choose to include in your message (such as your email address and any details you share about an issue).

I will use this information only to:

  • Reply to your question or support request
  • Improve the app based on anonymised feedback

If you want any email you sent (and associated data) to be deleted, you can request this in a follow-up email.

Children’s privacy

Lofi Pomodoro is suitable for general audiences but is not specifically designed for children. The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Because no data is collected from any user, there are no tracking or profiling features for children.

Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy ever changes (for example, if the app adds optional online features), the text will be updated here and the App Store privacy information will be adjusted accordingly. Any change that involves collecting data will be clearly explained, and you will always be able to choose whether to use new features that require data.
